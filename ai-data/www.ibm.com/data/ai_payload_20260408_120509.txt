                                                Introducción
                                            




Seeking an experienced procurement sourcing manager for direct material sourcing within healthcare and life sciences industry. This role focuses on helping clients with strategic and tactical sourcing, supplier management, cost optimization, and compliance with regulatory standards (FDA, GMP, ISO).









                                                Su función y responsabilidades
                                            




Strategic Sourcing:
 Develop and implement sourcing strategies for APIs, excipients, medical-grade materials, and packaging components.
Market Analysis & Cost Optimization:
 Perform market research and drive cost-reduction initiatives through competitive bidding and supplier consolidation.
Supplier & Contract Management:
 Manage global supplier relationships, negotiate agreements, and ensure compliance with healthcare regulations.
Procurement Leadership:
 Oversee R&D procurement, commodity and category management, contract administration, and should-cost analysis.
Regulatory Compliance & Risk Management:
 Ensure adherence to FDA, GMP, and ISO standards while mitigating supply chain risks.
Cross-Functional Collaboration:
 Partner with R&D, manufacturing, quality, and regulatory teams to support product launches and lifecycle management.
Technology & Analytics:
 Leverage ERP systems (SAP, Ariba) and procurement analytics for spend analysis, forecasting, and supplier performance monitoring.
Reporting & Insights:
 Prepare dashboards and reports for leadership review; deliver market intelligence and actionable insights for client sourcing strategies.
End-to-End Sourcing Execution:
 Manage sourcing projects from requirement gathering through supplier selection, negotiation, and contract finalization.
Operational Support:
 Provide analytical and operational support for sourcing initiatives, including RFx processes and supplier evaluations.
Contract Lifecycle Management:
 Administer contracts from drafting and redlining to compliance checks and renewals.

Supplier Relationship Management

Contract & IP Management
