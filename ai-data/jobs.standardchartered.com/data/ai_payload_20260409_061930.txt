Job Description: 
        


Job Summary
 
This role could be based in India and China. When you start the application process you will be presented with a drop down menu showing all countries, Please ensure that you select a country where the role is based.


Principal Solution Architect – is responsible for overseeing the solution architecture for the assigned domains
•    Have a deep understanding of the relevant domains, focused on digital channels, Mobile, Web and AI
•    Good understanding of underlying foundational technologies including cloud, resiliency, security and identity
•    Drive technical solutions for platforms across these domains
•    Lead the strategy, architecture and roadmaps
•    Collaborate with business and technology stakeholders to ensure that technology strategy, priorities are relevant to business strategy and objectives
•    Contribute to uplifting architecture practice in all aspects including resiliency, security, reduction of complexity and performance, as well as architecture governance for these domains
•    Enforce Enterprise Architecture Policy expectations 
•    Ensure regulatory and audit expectations and requirements from Architecture & cloud adoption perspective
•    Ensure an innovative and sustainable enterprise
•    Create intentional architecture to be nimble aligned with cloud transformation agenda
•    Manage holistically obsolescence and vulnerability
•    Lead principle driven management of security, reliability, resiliency, and recoverability
Key Responsibilities
 
Strategy
•    Develop and drive technology strategy aligned to both Business and Group’s Tech strategy 
•    Provide architecture direction, guiding principles, guardrails and roadmaps that are aligned to both Business and Group’s Tech strategy
•    Govern the tech decisions, and architecture directions across portfolio of change 
•    Accelerate the Cloud Value strategy through architecting strong foundational building blocks and reusable Cloud patterns.
•    Provide directions of SaaS based products where it makes technical and commercial sense to migrate from on-prem products.
•    Drive Adoption of Information and Cyber Security controls delivered by Cyber Security Services globally across the Consumer, Private and Business Banking (WRB) portfolio.
•    Strategy to increasing productivity of our cloud architecture & engineering through providing them the right tools, career paths and collaboration capabilities. 
•    Improve developer productivity through creation Infrastructure as Code, Cloud patterns which are engineered well and used by development teams across the bank accelerating speed to market.


Business
•    Enable business to increase revenue where speed to market is crucial – by modernising, reducing the complexity of the technology landscape and transforming technology assets to fit-for-purpose, flexible and cloud ready platforms that supports business innovations required to enable aggressive acquisition & retention in the targeted segments & markets.
•    Improve cost efficiency – drive efficiency by identifying opportunities to rationalise to a fewer best of breed platforms which provides strategic business capability and aggressively decommission redundant applications. 
•    Collaborate with various Business, Technology and Security Teams.
•    Govern the architecture decisions, and directions across the bespoke domain
•    Bring thought leadership in assessing and defining architecture and design solutions for cloud


Processes
•    Develop architecture blueprint for transformation agenda including cloud following Architecture Management standards and processes
•    Facilitate Architecture governance and approvals
 
•    Work with Architecture, Security, Compliance, Cloud and Technology Governance to follow appropriate policies, procedures and controls 


People & Talent
•    People leadership responsibility, set and monitor job descriptions and objectives for direct reports and provide feedback and rewards in line with their performance against those responsibilities and objectives.
•    Lead through example and build the appropriate culture and values.  Set appropriate tone and expectations from the team.
•    Ensure the provision of ongoing training and development; make sure that the team members are suitably skilled and qualified for their roles, ensuring that they have effective supervision in place to mitigate any risks.
•    Employ, engage and retain talents with diversity agenda in mind.


Risk Management
•    Ensure a full understanding of the risk and control environment 
•    Ensure onboarding of technologies are done properly with Legal, Compliance and Security Teams
•    Act quickly and decisively when any risk and control weakness become apparent and ensure they are addressed within an appropriate timeframe and, if necessary, escalated through the relevant committees


Governance
•    Ensure adherence to, bank-wide technology, cloud, security and architecture standards.
•    Awareness and understanding of the regulatory framework, in which the Group operates, and the regulatory requirements and expectations relevant to the role.
•    Improve and simplify architecture and cloud governance and decisioning across the strategic Technology Themes


Regulatory & Business Conduct
Cross
•    Display exemplary conduct and live by the Group’s Values and Code of Conduct. 
•    Take personal responsibility for embedding the highest standards of ethics, including regulatory and business conduct, across Standard Chartered Bank. This includes understanding and ensuring compliance with, in letter and spirit, all applicable laws, regulations, guidelines and the Group Code of Conduct.
•    Effectively and collaboratively identify, escalate, mitigate and resolve risk, conduct and compliance matters.
 
Key stakeholders
•    CIO, WRB
•    CIO Technology Platforms & Architecture
•    Chief Architect, WRB
•    Global Head of Platforms/Journeys Delivery, WRB
•    Platform Domain Tech Leads
•    Enterprise/Domain Architecture Teams, Enterprise Technology Team, Technology Standards Team
•    Business Domain Heads
•    Cloud Engineering, Cyber Security and Compliance teams


Other Responsibilities
•    Embed Here for Good and Group’s brand and values in first line operational risk management.


Key Requirements for the role:
•    Architecture and Problem-Solving ability
•    Deep knowledge of Mobile and Web Technologies AND/OR deep business domain knowledge in one or more of payments, wealth, Retail Banking
•    Usage of AI in Business solutions and Architecture
•    Experience in Financial Services or adjacent industries, or tech sector
Skills and Experience
 


Expertise in Architecture Modelling techniques, leveraging frameworks to visualise and communicate complex system designs.


Strong analytical abilities in Requirements Analysis, ensuring solutions clearly address business and technical needs.


Comprehensive knowledge of Platform Architecture, including designing scalable and resilient infrastructure components.


Proficient understanding of Software Product Technical intricacies, enabling integration and interoperability across systems.


Experience in Application Design and robust architectural practices to ensure high-quality, maintainable software solutions.


Familiarity with Business Architecture to align technology implementations with strategic business goals.


Depth in Data Architecture concepts, emphasising data management, governance, and effective utilisation within solutions.


Expertise in Information Security Architecture to embed security controls and practices within architectural designs.


Strong foundation in overall IT Architecture strategies, balancing innovation with practical implementation feasibility.


Capability in Transitional Architecture to guide phased migration and transformation initiatives ensuring business continuity.


Domain experience in Wealth Management services and Retail Banking technologies, providing context-specific architectural insights.


 


The ideal candidate will hold at minimum a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or a closely related field.


At least 12+ years in total, encompassing a minimum of 8 years dedicated to banking technology, enterprise architecture, or steering comprehensive digital transformation projects within financial services.


Exceptional communication skills in English are required, alongside proficiency in any local languages pertinent to the candidate's country of employment. Such language skills enhance collaboration across multicultural teams and enable effective stakeholder engagement.


Enterprise architecture frameworks (such as TOGAF, Zachman) are highly advantageous and demonstrate a commitment to maintaining professional excellence.
