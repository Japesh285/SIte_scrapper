Job Description: 
        


Job Summary
 
• Requirement analysis, setting up data processes / logics and covert existing datasets to the required schemas as per new tool’s requirements 
• Testing & reconciliations, closing data gaps, work with technology for production deployment, 
• User inquiry management and trainings, adoption, 
• Enhancement and change management, etc.
• Ensuring clarity around priorities and goals for the entire functional area.
• Manage projects with strong technical and data driven components
• Work closely with the multiple upstream & downstream teams on program setup and testing framework/tactics
• Provide compilation of analysis and results in presentation format, complete with timely and actionable recommendations for implementation
Key Responsibilities
 
Processes
• SBIM - Retail Analytics Opserations


People & Talent 
• Band 6 – Senior Manager


Regulatory & Business Conduct
• Display exemplary conduct and live by the Group’s Values and Code of Conduct. 
• Take personal responsibility for embedding the highest standards of ethics, including regulatory and business conduct, across Standard Chartered Bank. This includes understanding and ensuring compliance with, in letter and spirit, all applicable laws, regulations, guidelines and the Group Code of Conduct.
• Effectively and collaboratively identify, escalate, mitigate and resolve risk, conduct and compliance matters.
• Lead to achieve the outcomes set out in the Bank’s Conduct Principles: [Fair Outcomes for Clients; Effective Financial Markets; Financial Crime Compliance; The Right Environment.] 
• Serve as a Director of the Board 
• Exercise authorities delegated by the Board of Directors and act in accordance with Articles of Association (or equivalent) 


Key stakeholders
• Multiple stakeholder


Other Responsibilities
• Embed Here for good and Group’s brand and values in  [GBS India / SBIM]; Perform other responsibilities assigned under Group, Country, Business or Functional policies and procedures; Multiple functions (double hats)
• Banking and financial services ( advantageous if they have worked on wealth products and or Affluent/High net worth/Premier segments). 
• Experience working on finance tech (Hyperion, TM1, finsight etc is also preferrable)
• Broad experience, across functional and technical areas, designing, developing and deploying large scale projects.
• Proven experience in building high performance data team.
• Should have strong data understanding skills which helps multiple teams to support their requirements.
 
• Ability to create and deliver effective client management
• Define & implement resourcing strategy, involve in hiring of right talent.
• Developing and executing long term strategy and implementation plan for scaling the business 
• Understanding the requirements from leadership and transforming into executable implementation plan
• Should be able to drive, mentor and guide team larger team, should have team and people management experience.
• Strong banking knowledge especially in retail and wealth segments. 
• Should be able to manage BAU team and should be hands-on when required to deliver critical & timebound projects.
Qualifications
 
Mandatory 
• 5-7+ years’ experience in SQL/HQL (Data manipulation skills – created, maintained, retrieved tables )
• 5+ years’ Experience in Python
• Experience working in PySpark
• Working knowledge in Open source, Dataiku/Datarobot, hue etc
• Expertise in MS Excel ( VBA will be an added advantage )


Optional  
• Working knowledge of “Dataiku” platform through pySPark (or) Spark SQL
• Working knowledge on AI Tools
• Domain knowledge in Banking and/(or) financial sector
Skills and Experience
 
• SQL/ HQL 
• Python
• Dataiku / Databricks / pySPark
• Excellent numerical aptitude
• Ability to interpret complex data sets and extract insight
• Analytical technology and statistical tools
• BI tools
• AI tools
About Standard Chartered
 
We're an international bank, nimble enough to act, big enough for impact. For more than 170 years, we've worked to make a positive difference for our clients, communities, and each other. We question the status quo, love a challenge and enjoy finding new opportunities to grow and do better than before. If you're looking for a career with purpose and you want to work for a bank making a difference, we want to hear from you. You can count on us to celebrate your unique talents and we can't wait to see the talents you can bring us.


Our purpose, to drive commerce and prosperity through our unique diversity, together with our brand promise, to be here for good are achieved by how we each live our valued behaviours. When you work with us, you'll see how we value difference and advocate inclusion.

Never settle,

Flexible working

Proactive wellbeing support

A continuous learning culture

Being part of an inclusive and values driven organisation,
