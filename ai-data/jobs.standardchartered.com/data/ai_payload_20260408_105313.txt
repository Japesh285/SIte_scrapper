Job Description: 
        


Job Summary
 
The Strategy & Planning Lead will be working within the TPS-ICS domain to s
upport the definition of CISO strategy (goals and book of work) for CIB, Core Technology and Functions CISO and WRB and Markets CISO with alignment to the Bank, Business, T&O and ICS strategy.
Key Responsibilities
 
 






Support ongoing identification of efficiencies to meet T&O and RoTE targets.


Deliver operational and cost optimisation per ICS Strategy and Roadmap. Meet ICS budget targets.


Manage the CISO Portfolio within allocated budget.


Track and manage cost and HC budgets to meet targets.


Conduct detailed efficiency and optimisation analysis.


Produce documentation for ICS MT on-sites and workshops.


Support leadership in Strategy & Planning.


Prepare content for team meetings, strategy day, and senior management.


Prepare domain content for ICS Townhalls.


Draft and consolidate key announcement communications.


Build relationships with ICS Strategy, Financials, Service Management, and key stakeholders.


Perform monthly HC validation including roles, spans, layers, and Job Family framework.


Track and drive completion of MyVoice actions with regular updates.


Support CIB, Core Technology, Functions, WRB, and Markets CISOs in performance assessments and year-end.


Help CISOs build team bench strength for sustained ICS risk improvements.
