Optum is a global organization that delivers care, aided by technology to help millions of people live healthier lives. The work you do with our team will directly improve health outcomes by connecting people with the care, pharmacy benefits, data and resources they need to feel their best. Here, you will find a culture guided by inclusion, talented peers, comprehensive benefits and career development opportunities. Come make an impact on the communities we serve as you help us advance health optimization on a global scale. Join us to start 
Caring. Connecting. Growing together.
Primary Responsibilities:
Firewall Administration: Configure, manage, and troubleshoot Palo Alto Firewalls to secure our organization's network
Security Policies: Develop and enforce network security policies, including NAT, VPN, IPS/IDS, and access control policies
Threat Prevention: Deploy and maintain features like anti-virus, anti-spyware, and vulnerability protection on Palo Alto firewalls
Monitoring and Reporting: Continuously monitor firewall traffic and logs, generating reports to identify potential security threats or areas for improvement
Network Security Solutions: Recommend and implement enhancements to improve overall network security posture
Incident Response: Respond to and resolve network security incidents in a timely manner
Collaboration: Work with other IT teams to integrate firewall solutions with existing network infrastructure
Documentation: Maintain accurate documentation of firewall configurations, policies, and security incidents
Configure, deploy, and manage Palo-Alto and Cisco ASA firewalls to secure the organization's network infrastructure. This involves setting up firewall rules, access control lists, and VPN tunnels, as well as performing regular firewall maintenance tasks
Monitor network traffic and security events using network security monitoring tools and platforms. Analyze logs and alerts generated by firewalls and other security devices to identify and respond to potential security incidents
Conduct periodic reviews of firewall rules and policies to ensure they align with the organization's security requirements and industry best practices. Remove unnecessary or outdated rules and update configurations as needed
Deploy and manage intrusion detection and prevention systems (IDS/IPS) to detect and prevent network-based attacks. Configure and fine-tune these systems to identify and block malicious activities
Perform regular vulnerability assessments and scans on the network infrastructure to identify potential security weaknesses. Coordinate with system administrators and other stakeholders to remediate vulnerabilities and ensure a secure network environment
Respond to security incidents promptly, investigating and containing the threats. Collaborate with other teams to mitigate the impact of security incidents, restore services, and implement preventive measures to minimize future incidents
Develop and enforce network security policies and procedures in line with industry standards and regulations. Ensure compliance with relevant security frameworks such as ISO 27001, NIST, and PCI DSS
Participate in security audits and assessments to evaluate the effectiveness of network security controls. Implement necessary changes and improvements to maintain compliance and security
Maintain accurate documentation of network security configurations, incidents, and procedures. Generate regular reports on network security metrics, vulnerabilities, incidents, and compliance status for management and stakeholders
Keep up to date with the latest network security threats, vulnerabilities, and industry best practices. Continuously enhance technical knowledge and skills through training, certifications, and participation in security communities and forums
Work collaboratively with cross-functional teams, including system administrators, network engineers, and IT management, to ensure a holistic approach to network security. Communicate effectively to stakeholders about network security risks, incidents, and mitigation strategies
Comply with the terms and conditions of the employment contract, company policies and procedures, and any and all directives (such as, but not limited to, transfer and/or re-assignment to different work locations, change in teams and/or work shifts, policies in regards to flexibility of work benefits and/or work environment, alternative work arrangements, and other decisions that may arise due to the changing business environment). The Company may adopt, vary or rescind these policies and directives in its absolute discretion and without any limitation (implied or otherwise) on its ability to do so
Required Qualifications:
Any bachelor's degree with 7+ years of experience
5+ years of experience working with Palo Alto Firewalls
Experience with network monitoring and logging tools
Solid knowledge and experience in configuring and managing Palo-Alto and Cisco ASA firewalls
Familiarity with network security protocols, technologies, and best practices
Deep understanding of network security protocols and technologies (SSL, IPSec, VPN, IDS/IPS, NAT)
Solid knowledge of firewall management platforms and Panorama (Palo Alto's centralized management system)
Familiarity with LAN/WAN infrastructure, including switches, routers, and network topology
Proficiency in network security tools, such as IDS/IPS, vulnerability scanners, and network monitoring systems
Proven solid communication and collaboration skills
Proven solid analytical and problem-solving skills
Proven excellent communication and collaboration abilities
Demonstrated ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks effectively
Preferred Qualifications:
Certifications like Palo Alto Networks Certified Network Security Engineer (PCNSE) are preferred
Relevant certifications such as Palo-Alto Certified Network Security Administrator (PCNSA) or Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) Security are a plus

We are committed to mitigating our impact on the environment and enabling and delivering equitable care that addresses health disparities and improves health outcomes - an enterprise priority reflected in our mission.

Fraudulent Activity Notice



      We have received recent reports of fraudulent LinkedIn messages and emails alleging or claiming to be sent from UnitedHealth Group, UnitedHealthcare, or Optum Executives.
    



      The fraudulent LinkedIn messages and emails, which do not originate from any Executives LinkedIn account or of UnitedHealth Group’s email domains, or those of any of its operating divisions, supposedly conducts an interview via a Zoom meeting, offers a work from home job at Optum, emails an application, sends a fake check by next day delivery through USPS and asks recipients to pay a vendor a large dollar amount. These counterfeit-check cashing schemes exist and use a variety of deceptions to get people to cash these fraudulent checks.
    



      UnitedHealth Group will never request you to pay a vendor or pay a fee of any sort to explore employment opportunities with our company.
    



      If you wish to verify the legitimacy of any email alleging or claiming to have been sent by or on behalf of UnitedHealth Group Executives or Recruiters, please call 1-800-561-0861 between 7 a.m. and 7 p.m. CT, Monday – Friday, for assistance.
