Job Description:
 
Key Responsibilities:
 
 
Perform daily SAP BASIS administration tasks including monitoring, troubleshooting, performance tuning, and system health checks for SAP systems on HANA DB.
 
Install, configure, upgrade, and maintain SAP S/4HANA E4H landscapes.
 
Manage HANA database administration tasks such as backup/recovery, replication setup, performance optimization, and housekeeping.
 
Support SAP BTP integrations, connectivity, and extensions in coordination with development and architecture teams.
 
Perform system copies, refreshes, and client administration as per project and operational needs.
 
Ensure high availability and disaster recovery readiness for SAP HANA databases and application components.
 
Coordinate with SAP support for OSS incidents and implement recommended solutions.
 
Apply support packages, SAP notes, kernel upgrades, and security patches as required.
 
Support SAP E4H application teams with technical issues, transports, and change requests.
 
Document technical configurations, operational procedures, and changes effectively.
 
Work closely with application, infrastructure, security, and cloud teams for integrated support.
 
Participate in on-call support rotations for critical issues resolution.
