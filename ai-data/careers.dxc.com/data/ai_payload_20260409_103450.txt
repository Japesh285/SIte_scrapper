Job Description:
 
 
Quality Management System (QMS)
 
 
Develop, implement, and maintain the organization’s Quality Management System (QMS) in line with ISO and other applicable standards.
 
Ensure compliance with regulatory, contractual, and customer requirements.
 
Define, monitor, and report key quality metrics, audits, and corrective actions.
 
Lead internal audits and coordinate external certification/recertification audits.
 
Drive continual improvement initiatives across processes, policies, and practices.
 
Train and mentor teams on QMS standards, documentation, and compliance.
 
 
Project Management Office (PMO)
 
 
Establish and manage PMO governance frameworks, project methodologies, and reporting structures.
 
Oversee planning, execution, and delivery of strategic and operational projects.
 
Ensure project alignment with organizational goals, budgets, and timelines.
 
Monitor risks, dependencies, and performance of projects across functions.
 
Provide executive dashboards, status reports, and insights for leadership decision-making.
 
Build project management capability across teams through coaching and training
 
 
Skills/Knowledge/Experience required
 
 
 
Strong understanding of 
ISO 9001, CMMI, Six Sigma, or other QMS frameworks
.
 
Proven expertise in 
Project Management methodologies
 (Agile, Waterfall, PRINCE2, PMI, etc.)
 
Monitoring process adherence, identifying risks, and implementing corrective and preventive actions (CAPA).
 
Driving standardization across processes to ensure consistency in error tracking, RCA, and reporting (including dashboards and insights to leadership and clients)
 
Excellent stakeholder management, influencing, and communication skills.
 
Strong analytical, problem-solving, and process improvement mindset.
 
Ability to balance compliance needs with business agility and efficiency.
 
 
Qualifications 
(describe the qualifications considered essential to do the job, e.g., Chartered Accountancy, MBA, etc)
 
 
 
Graduate/Post-Graduate
 
Certification(s):
 ISO Lead Auditor, PMP / PRINCE2, Lean Six Sigma (preferred).
 
8–12 years of experience in 
Quality Management, PMO, or Operations Excellence
, with at least 3–5 years in a managerial role.
 
Experience in leading audits, certifications, and enterprise-level projects.
 
Industry experience in BPO, ITES, BFSI
 
Certification in Life Insurance Domain will be an added advantage
