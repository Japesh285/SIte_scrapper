Job Description:
 
Senior DevOps Engineer – Azure, AKS, Automation & AI Enablement
 
Role Summary
 
We are seeking a highly skilled 
Senior DevOps Engineer
 with deep expertise in 
Azure infrastructure
, 
cloud networking
, and particularly 
Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS)
. The ideal candidate will have strong experience in 
Infrastructure‑as‑Code (IaC)
 using 
Terraform
 and working knowledge of 
Ansible
 for configuration management.
 
This role requires someone who is not only technically strong but also 
forward‑thinking
, with a mindset to 
experiment with AI
, adopt 
MCPs
, and use AI-driven automation to improve speed, reliability, and developer productivity across the engineering ecosystem.
 
The candidate must also have proven experience building and optimizing 
Azure DevOps (ADO) pipelines
, and familiarity with 
GitHub Actions
 is a strong plus.
 
 
Key Responsibilities
 
Cloud Infrastructure & AKS
 
 
Design, deploy, and maintain 
Azure infrastructure
 including VNets, subnets, firewalls, Application Gateways, Private Links, and NSGs.
 
Architect, configure, and optimize 
AKS clusters
, including node pools, autoscaling, workload identity, networking models, and cluster governance.
 
Implement secure cluster networking (CNI, Azure CNI overlay, ingress controllers).
 
 
Automation & Infrastructure-as-Code
 
 
Build and maintain reusable IaC modules using 
Terraform
 (preferred) and 
Ansible
.
 
Ensure consistent environment provisioning across dev, QA, staging, and production.
 
Implement GitOps-style workflows for deployments and infrastructure state management.
 
 
DevOps, CI/CD & Observability
 
 
Design, build, and maintain 
Azure DevOps pipelines
 for build, deploy, test, and security automation.
 
Contribute to OR lead migration or parallel adoption of 
GitHub Actions
 workflows.
 
Enhance observability with tooling such as 
Azure Monitor, Log Analytics, Prometheus/Grafana
, and distributed tracing solutions.
 
 
AI-Driven Engineering & MCP Automation
 
 
Identify opportunities to use 
AI agents, MCPs, and automation frameworks
 to accelerate deployments, detect issues earlier, or simplify operational processes.
 
Create AI-based workflows and scripts for faster infrastructure debugging, document generation, or environment provisioning.
 
Encourage and influence a culture of 
AI-first engineering experimentation
.
 
 
Security, Governance & Compliance
 
 
Implement best practices in 
Azure security
, including workload identity, RBAC, Key Vault, managed identities, and network isolation.
 
Collaborate with architecture, security, and infra teams to ensure compliance with enterprise standards.
 
 
 
Required Skills & Experience
 
Core Cloud & Kubernetes Skills
 
 
6–10+ years in DevOps, SRE, or cloud engineering roles.
 
Strong knowledge of 
Azure infrastructure
 (networking, compute, storage, identity).
 
Deep hands-on experience with 
AKS cluster architecture, operations, and scaling
.
 
Proficiency in 
containerization
 (Docker), container security, and Kubernetes networking.
 
 
Automation & IaC
 
 
Strong experience in 
Terraform
 for multi-environment IaC.
 
Working knowledge of 
Ansible
 for provisioning and configuration.
 
Solid understanding of GitOps principles (Argo CD or Flux is a plus).
 
 
CI/CD & Code Repositories
 
 
Strong experience building 
Azure DevOps pipelines
 (YAML-based preferred).
 
Knowledge of 
GitHub Actions
 and workflow optimization.
 
 
Scripting & Tooling
 
 
Proficiency in 
PowerShell
, 
Bash
, or 
Python
 scripting.
 
Experience with Helm, Kustomize, and Kubernetes package management.
 
 
AI Mindset & Continuous Innovation
 
 
Curiosity and drive to adopt 
AI/automation tools
 to enhance engineering velocity.
 
Ability to create or integrate AI-driven workflows and MCPs.
 
 
 
Preferred / Nice-to-Have
 
 
Experience implementing 
service mesh
 (Istio/Linkerd).
 
Exposure to 
Azure API Management (APIM)
.
 
Experience with cost optimization and FinOps practices.
 
Previous work with 
Azure Container Apps
, Functions, or event-driven architecture.
 
Understanding of SRE principles (SLIs, SLOs, error budgets).
 
 
 
Candidate Profile
 
This role is ideal for someone who:
 
 
Thrives in modern cloud-native environments.
 
Can own end-to-end DevOps engineering for large-scale distributed systems.
 
Has a 
builder and automator mindset
, especially using AI tools.
 
Enjoys improving engineering workflows and reducing toil.
 
Stays updated on DevOps, Kubernetes, and AI automation trends.
