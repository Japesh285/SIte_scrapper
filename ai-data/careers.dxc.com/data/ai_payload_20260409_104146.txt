Location:

Job Description:
 
Role/ Responsibilities:
 
• Assist in design and development of the account application
 
• Influencing peers, juniors, and seniors both within the organization and across the account.
 
• Coordinate and collaborate with the Product and Engineering teams to understand and solve problems, come up with creative solutions, and help with tracking and delivering within the release plan
 
• Collaborate with Engineering and QA to resolve bugs
 
Required Skills and Experience: 
 
 
8+ years’ experience in IT
 
5+ years as Sr DevOps, with AWS hands-on experience
 
7+ years CI/CD experience
 
1+ Years experience in technical leadership role
 
Experience infrastructure provisioning using Terraforms, CloudFormation
 
Experience doing production support and 
able to guide the team
.
 
Hands on experience in AWS provisioning and good knowledge of AWS Services like ECS – Fargate, OpenSearch.
 
Knowledge of CloudFormation scripts for automation purpose.
 
Experience in working on GitHub and GitHub actions.
 
Experience in Monitoring the Kibana / DataDog / Dynatrace and checking the latency issues if any.
 
Akamai – Understanding 
 
 
Preferred additional Skills
 
 
Experience in creating of Elastic Cache – Redis cluster and monitoring the metrics. 
 
Experience in DR setup (Active / Active or Active / Passive)
 
Hands on experience in AWS provisioning and good knowledge of AWS Services like ECS
 
Experience in creating of DynamoDB tables, DocumentDB, and AuroraDB.
 
Harness
 
Experience in Route 53
 
 
Soft Skills
 
 
The candidate should have excellent communication skills. Verbal communication should be clear, as well as written skills using US English.
 
The candidate should be capable of quickly grasping high-level concepts and can communicate them to others.
 
The candidate can work on a fast-moving development team, be candid about the status of their work with the development team.
 
The candidate cares about quality.
 
Positive attitude
