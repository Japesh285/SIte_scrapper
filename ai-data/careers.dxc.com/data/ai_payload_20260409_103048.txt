Job Description:
 
Key Responsibilities:
 
 
Administer and support 
Active Directory infrastructure
, including Domain Controllers, OU management, Group Policy (GPO), DNS, and DHCP.
 
Manage and troubleshoot 
authentication mechanisms
 such as 
Kerberos, NTLM, and LDAP
.
 
Implement and support 
Single Sign-On (SSO)
 solutions using 
ADFS, SAML, and federation technologies
.
 
Manage and maintain 
Azure Active Directory (Azure AD / Entra ID)
 services, including identity synchronization, access policies, and security configurations.
 
Develop and maintain 
PowerShell scripts and automation
 to streamline operational tasks and improve efficiency.
 
Manage 
Windows Server and Windows client operating systems
 within enterprise environments.
 
Troubleshoot identity, authentication, and directory-related issues across 
on-premises and cloud platforms
.
 
Collaborate with security, infrastructure, and application teams to ensure proper 
identity and access management
 practices.
 
Ensure adherence to 
security policies, access control standards, and compliance requirements
.
