SAP Senior ABAP Consultant
 
 
 
Job ID:
 51572521
 
Category:
 
Software Engineering
 
Employment Type:
 
Full time
 
 
 
 
Apply

Location:
 Hyderabad, India 
 
 
Job Description:
 
A Senior SAP ABAP Developer designs, builds, and maintains complex custom solutions, leading development, mentoring juniors, and ensuring high performance, often using modern tech like ABAP Cloud, SAPUI5/Fiori, and RAP (RESTful ABAP Programming Model) for S/4HANA projects, translating business needs into technical specs, collaborating across teams, and supporting full lifecycle development with a focus on quality and innovation in cloud environments. 
 
Key Responsibilities
 
 
 
Development:
 Create, enhance, and maintain ABAP programs, reports, enhancements, and interfaces (BAPIs, OData, APIs).
 
 
 
Modern Tech:
 Develop UIs with SAPUI5/Fiori and build services using RAP and SAP BTP.
 
 
 
Collaboration:
 Work closely with functional consultants, architects, and business owners to define requirements and solutions.
 
 
 
Leadership & Mentoring:
 Guide junior developers, conduct peer reviews, and provide technical expertise.
 
 
 
Lifecycle Management:
 Participate in all phases: analysis, design, coding, testing (unit/integration), deployment, and support.
 
 
 
Optimization:
 Improve code performance, scalability, and maintainability.
 
 
 
Documentation:
 Prepare technical and functional documentation. 
 
 
 
Key Skills & Qualifications
 
 
 
Experience:
 8+ years in SAP ABAP development, ideally with S/4HANA.
 
 
 
Technical:
 Strong ABAP Objects, ALV, Enhancements, Forms, BAPIs, BADIs, OData, SAP Gateway, SAP BTP, Fiori.
 
 
 
Methodologies:
 Experience with agile (SAFe) and SAP's Clean Core approach.
 
 
 
Soft Skills:
 Strong analytical, problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills.
