Lead Snowflake IDMC Architect
 
Job Location: Hyderabad / Bangalore / Chennai / Kolkata / Noida/ Gurgaon / Pune / Indore / Mumbai
 
Experience: 11+ Years
 
 
About the Role
 
We are seeking an experienced Snowflake IDMC Architect with strong hands-on expertise in Informatica Intelligent Data Management Cloud (IDMC / IICS). The ideal candidate will design, develop, and optimize cloud-based data integration solutions. Experience with Snowflake and dbt is a plus.
 
Key Responsibilities
 
•            Design, build, and maintain IDMC/IICS data integration pipelines, mappings, and taskflows.
 
•            Develop scalable ETL/ELT workflows for ingestion and transformation from diverse data sources.
 
•            Configure and manage Secure Agents, connections, runtime environments, and integration tasks.
 
•            Optimize performance of mappings and troubleshoot issues in development & production.
 
•            Work with data owners, architects, and cross-functional teams to understand and translate requirements into technical solutions.
 
•            Ensure adherence to data governance, quality, and best practices.
 
•            Participate in deployment processes and support release cycles.
 
•            Provide L2/L3 support, root cause analysis, and enhancements to existing integrations.
