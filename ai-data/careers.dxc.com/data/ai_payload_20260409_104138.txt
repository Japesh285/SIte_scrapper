Job Description:
 
Key Responsibilities
 
FinOps Leadership & Cloud Cost Optimization
 
 
 
Lead FinOps delivery across client engagements, ensuring continuous cost optimization and governance.
 
 
 
Identify, track, and drive 
FinOps savings
 to deliver tangible client benefits.
 
 
 
Develop and implement cloud cost visibility, reporting, budgeting, and forecasting practices.
 
 
 
Client Engagement & Governance
 
 
 
Manage 
client visits
, QBRs, governance meetings, and executive reviews.
 
 
 
Act as a trusted advisor to clients on cloud financial management and operational efficiency.
 
 
 
Present optimization insights, savings reports, and architectural recommendations in a clear, business‑aligned manner.
 
 
 
Cloud Operations & Architecture Alignment
 
 
 
Partner closely with 
Cloud Operations and Architecture
 teams to ensure cost optimization aligns with platform strategy and cloud roadmaps.
 
 
 
Translate FinOps insights into executable infrastructure and application improvement actions.
 
 
 
Evaluate cloud architecture for cost implications, scalability, performance, and operational efficiency.
 
 
 
RFPs, Solutioning & New Deal Support
 
 
 
Contribute significantly to 
RFP responses
, proposals, and presales discussions.
 
 
 
Support 
new deal solutioning
, including cloud cost models, sizing estimates, and optimization strategies.
 
 
 
Provide domain expertise to improve deal competitiveness and win rates.
 
 
 
Collaboration with Hyperscaler Offering Leads
 
 
 
Work closely with 
Azure and AWS offering leads
 on solution alignment, funding programs, architecture best practices, and go‑to‑market initiatives.
 
 
 
Leverage hyperscaler tools and partner benefits to enhance client outcomes and delivery efficiency.
 
 
 
Required Skills & Experience
 
 
 
7–12 years of experience in Cloud Engineering, FinOps, Cloud Operations, or Cloud Architecture.
 
 
 
Strong understanding of 
FinOps principles
, cloud cost governance, allocation models, and optimization methodologies.
 
 
 
Experience with AWS, Azure, or multi‑cloud environments and associated cost‑management tools.
 
 
 
Proven experience in client‑facing delivery roles, conducting governance discussions, and managing executive expectations.
 
 
 
Hands‑on understanding of cloud infrastructure (compute, storage, network), architectural patterns, and cloud-native operations.
 
 
 
Experience supporting 
RFPs, solutioning, and presales engagements
.
 
 
 
Excellent communication, stakeholder management, and presentation skills.
 
 
 
Ability to collaborate with cross‑functional teams including Cloud Ops, Architecture, and hyperscaler partner teams.
 
 
 
Preferred Qualifications
 
 
 
FinOps Certified Practitioner or Professional
 
 
 
Azure or AWS Cloud Certifications
 
 
 
Experience in multi‑cloud cost management platforms (CloudHealth, Cloudability, Azure Cost Management, AWS Cost Explorer, etc.)
 
 
 
Strong analytical skills with an ability to translate financial data into actionable insights.
