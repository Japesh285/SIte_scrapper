Job Description:
 
Summary
 
 
Perform and work with team on monthly payroll output to completely engage with employee flexi reimbursement claim validation and investment proof validation activity. Ensure, the assigned tasks are completed within the stipulated timeline, co-ordinate with various payroll stakeholder and ensure all the required inputs are received on time.
 
 
Essential Job Functions
 
 
 
Ensure the fundamental payroll knowledge
 
Ensure the policies and Income Tax Act with regard to employee flexi reimbursement
 
Ensure the knowledge in employee flexi reimbursements claim validation as per statutory compliance
 
Ensure the knowledge in all the company policies related to flexi reimbursements
 
Statutory forms like PF, Tax etc.,
 
Employee investment declaration & proofs validation activities
 
Supporting employee payroll queries through service now tool
 
Provide apt resolution on time for queries
 
Connecting employees through teams call in case employee required virtual support
 
Ability to handle the situation of escalations
 
 
 
Basic Qualifications
 
 
 
Bachelor's degree in business administration, finance, accounting or related field preferred
 
2 to 3 years of experience in India payroll support
 
Strong knowledge in Payroll function
 
Experience in flexi reimbursement policies and Income Tax Act with regard to employee investment proofs
 
Knowledge in SAP, Workday and Excel
 
 
Other Qualifications
 
 
 
Good communication skills to interact with employees and senior management
 
Good interpersonal skills for communicating with other departments and answering employee questions
 
Good planning and organizational skills to balance and prioritize work
 
Good presentation skills
 
Good analytical and problem-solving skills
 
Ability to work independently and as part of a team
