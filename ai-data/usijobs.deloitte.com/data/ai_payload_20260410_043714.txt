Assistant Manager, Global Assurance Team
 
Role Description
The Assistant Manager will be a core member of Deloitte’s Global Assurance team, supporting the execution of high-impact global initiatives that drive growth and enablement for AI assurance capabilities across Deloitte member firms. The role blends market activation, offering development, revenue and pipeline analytics, global community support, and PMO (project management office) delivery, requiring strong, structured thinking, stakeholder management, and executive-ready communications.
This professional will work in a fast-paced environment, supporting senior leaders through analysis, synthesis, and the delivery of strategic outputs while maintaining operational rigor across multiple workstreams. The Global Assurance team is expanding its global footprint by offering innovative thinking and frameworks, engagement processes, and ensuring Deloitte member firms are equipped to grow in their respective regions. The resource will have opportunities to interact with senior leaders across the organization and will also assist in the execution of various special projects that have the potential to significantly impact our organization.
The professional will be a part of an internal strategy consulting team that influences strategic decision-making for the firm. The professional will need to develop a knowledge of the firm’s business and professional services market at the global level. The professional will be expected to lead and/or assist on a diverse range of projects involving analysis of quantitative and qualitative data, synthesize findings, produce executive presentation material, and demonstrate solid analytical and writing skills. The team’s projects are typically high-impact and high-visibility, aligned to the firm and leadership’s strategic priorities.
 
What you will do
Global Assurance market activation and growth strategy:
Support 
Global Assurance market activation
 by conducting internal and external research on market trends, competitors, member firm positioning, and client demand to shape strategies that scale the business globally and locally (as applicable).
Develop strategy artifacts (e.g., 
strategy decks, executive readouts, and business cases
) that translate market signals into clear priorities, investment choices, and measurable activation plans.
Stand up and support 
global working groups
 to align member firms on growth plays, differentiators, and deployment plans, ensuring follow-through from discussion to outputs.
Global Assurance community support:
Support a global community of 
300+ SMEs across 35+ geographies
 by responding to enablement needs and ad-hoc requests with speed and quality.
Prepare, curate, and/or source 
GTM materials, tools, assets, and learning & development resources
 to accelerate selling and delivery readiness across member firms.
Identify and mobilize SMEs for pursuits and client opportunities, helping connect the right expertise to the right need at the right time.
Facilitate global calls, webcasts, and working sessions across time zones, capturing actions, owners, and outcomes.
Strategic, executive-level PowerPoint development and leadership communications:
Develop compelling 
executive-ready PowerPoint presentations
 for senior leader webcasts, community calls, and executive meetings; synthesize complex inputs into crisp storylines, decisions, and actions.
Provide leadership decision support through structured analysis, clear narrative, and high-quality written communication.
PMO across multiple workstreams:
Operate as PMO support for the service area initiatives: maintain workplans, manage dependencies, track milestones, and drive closure on actions and deliverables.
Manage project development through lifecycle stages (e.g., 
market assessment, launch, sustainment
), coordinating stakeholders and ensuring delivery discipline.
Demonstrate strong program management capability and agility across concurrent priorities.
Service offering development, frameworks, and pursuit enablement:
Support the development and evolution of 
new and existing service offerings
 by synthesizing: what clients are asking for, what member firms are already selling, and what the regulatory/standards landscape requires and is trending toward.
Contribute to the creation and maintenance of 
frameworks, playbooks, methodologies, and supporting assets
, ensuring global consistency while enabling local tailoring.
Enable commercial execution by supporting 
pursuits, opportunities, and go-to-market materials
, coordinating inputs from subject matter experts and member firm teams.
Revenue, pipeline, and financial performance analysis:
Conduct revenue/pipeline and financial analyses to understand global performance, using internal finance platforms (e.g., Jupiter) and structured data collection from the global Assurance network.
Gather and validate pipeline signals and performance insights through 
1:1 touchpoints
 with member firms and global leadership.
Support external research for 
market sizing
 and growth opportunity assessments for the service area and synthesize findings into leadership-ready insights.
Ad-hoc support:
Respond promptly to Global and Member Firm leadership requests with 
quick turnaround
, prioritizing effectively, and maintaining high-quality outputs under ambiguity.
Flex between strategic and operational tasks, adjusting quickly as priorities shift.
